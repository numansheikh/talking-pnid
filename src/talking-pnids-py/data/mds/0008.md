SECTION 1: MAIN EQUIPMENT SPECIFICATIONS
1.1 Fuel Gas Knockout Drum (PP01-362-V001)
The central process vessel is a horizontal knockout drum designed to remove entrained liquids from fuel gas streams originating from the DS1 and DS3 scrapper receivers. The vessel separates liquid droplets from the gas phase through gravity settling and velocity reduction before the gas proceeds to the Fuel Gas Treatment Package.
1.1.1 Equipment Identification
Parameter	Value
Equipment Tag	PP01-362-V001
Operating Tag	362-V001
Service	Fuel Gas Knockout Drum
Vessel Type	Horizontal Cylindrical Drum
1.1.2 Dimensional Data
Parameter	Value
Internal Diameter (ID)	2200 mm
Length (Tangent to Tangent)	5500 mm
Orientation	Horizontal
1.1.3 Design Conditions
Parameter	Value
Design Pressure	14 barg
Design Temperature	-20°C to 100°C (NOTE-7)
Operating Pressure	9.0 to 10.4 barg
Operating Temperature	23°C to 72°C
1.1.4 Material and Construction
Parameter	Value
Shell Material	Carbon Steel (CS)
Corrosion Allowance	3 mm
Internal Coating	Epoxy Ceramic Coating
External Insulation	None
Trim Number	PP01-362-V001-TX0001-B03F9
1.1.5 Vessel Internals
The vessel contains a mist eliminator designated as MW 24" (24-inch Mist Wire mesh demister pad). This wire mesh pad is positioned in the gas flow path upstream of the gas outlet nozzle N2. The demister captures entrained liquid droplets from the gas stream before the gas exits the vessel, improving separation efficiency.

SECTION 7: CONTROL LOOP DESCRIPTIONS
7.1 Pressure Control Loop (PIC-0002 → PV-0002)
Purpose: Maintains vessel operating pressure within 9.0-10.4 barg range by modulating gas flow to HP Flare.
Loop Components:
1.	PIT-0002: Pressure transmitter at Nozzle N16 senses vessel pressure
2.	PIC-0002: Controller compares measured pressure to setpoint
3.	PV-0002: 8-inch control valve on line 8"-PP01-362-GF0003-B01E9-PP modulates gas flow
Control Action:
•	Increasing vessel pressure → PIC-0002 output increases → PV-0002 opens → More gas flows to flare → Pressure decreases
•	Decreasing vessel pressure → PIC-0002 output decreases → PV-0002 closes → Less gas flows to flare → Pressure increases
7.2 Level Control Loop (LIC-0001 → LV-0001)
Purpose: Maintains vessel liquid level at Normal Liquid Level (800mm) by modulating liquid discharge.
Loop Components:
1.	LIT-0001: Level transmitter at Nozzles N7A/N7B senses liquid level
2.	LIC-0001: Controller compares measured level to NLL setpoint (800mm)
3.	LV-0001: 1.5-inch control valve on N3 outlet bypass modulates liquid flow
Control Action:
•	Rising level → LIC-0001 output increases → LV-0001 opens → More liquid drains → Level decreases
•	Falling level → LIC-0001 output decreases → LV-0001 closes → Less liquid drains → Level increases
7.3 High Pressure Safety Instrumented Function (PZT → 2oo3 → ESD)
Purpose: Provides independent high-pressure protection to prevent vessel overpressure.
Loop Components:
1.	PZT-0001A, PZT-0001B, PZT-0001C: Three independent pressure transmitters
2.	2oo3 Voting Logic (362-004): Requires 2 of 3 transmitters to confirm high pressure
3.	ESD System: Initiates protective actions

7.4 Level Safety Instrumented Function (LZT → 2oo3 → ESD)
Purpose: Provides independent level protection for both high-high and low-low level conditions.

1.	LZT-0002A, LZT-0002B, LZT-0002C: Three independent GWR level transmitters
2.	2oo3 Voting Logic (362-003 for HHLL, 362-007 for LLLL): Requires 2 of 3 transmitters to confirm level alarm

•	LLLL (300mm): Voting logic 362-007 trips to prevent pump cavitation or loss of seal

SECTION 7: CONTROL LOOP DESCRIPTIONS
7.1 Pressure Control Loop (PIC-0002 → PV-0002)
Purpose: Maintains vessel operating pressure within 9.0-10.4 barg range by modulating gas flow to HP Flare.
Loop Components:
1.	PIT-0002: Pressure transmitter at Nozzle N16 senses vessel pressure
2.	PIC-0002: Controller compares measured pressure to setpoint
3.	PV-0002: 8-inch control valve on line 8"-PP01-362-GF0003-B01E9-PP modulates gas flow
Control Action:
•	Increasing vessel pressure → PIC-0002 output increases → PV-0002 opens → More gas flows to flare → Pressure decreases
•	Decreasing vessel pressure → PIC-0002 output decreases → PV-0002 closes → Less gas flows to flare → Pressure increases
7.2 Level Control Loop (LIC-0001 → LV-0001)
Purpose: Maintains vessel liquid level at Normal Liquid Level (800mm) by modulating liquid discharge.
Loop Components:
1.	LIT-0001: Level transmitter at Nozzles N7A/N7B senses liquid level
2.	LIC-0001: Controller compares measured level to NLL setpoint (800mm)
3.	LV-0001: 1.5-inch control valve on N3 outlet bypass modulates liquid flow
Control Action:
•	Rising level → LIC-0001 output increases → LV-0001 opens → More liquid drains → Level decreases
•	Falling level → LIC-0001 output decreases → LV-0001 closes → Less liquid drains → Level increases
7.3 High Pressure Safety Instrumented Function (PZT → 2oo3 → ESD)
Purpose: Provides independent high-pressure protection to prevent vessel overpressure.
Loop Components:
1.	PZT-0001A, PZT-0001B, PZT-0001C: Three independent pressure transmitters
2.	2oo3 Voting Logic (362-004): Requires 2 of 3 transmitters to confirm high pressure
3.	ESD System: Initiates protective actions
Safety Action: When 2 out of 3 transmitters detect pressure above the high-pressure trip setpoint, the voting logic sends a trip signal to the ESD system, which closes EZV-0001 and initiates other protective actions.
7.4 Level Safety Instrumented Function (LZT → 2oo3 → ESD)
Purpose: Provides independent level protection for both high-high and low-low level conditions.
Loop Components:
1.	LZT-0002A, LZT-0002B, LZT-0002C: Three independent GWR level transmitters
2.	2oo3 Voting Logic (362-003 for HHLL, 362-007 for LLLL): Requires 2 of 3 transmitters to confirm level alarm
3.	ESD System: Initiates protective actions
Safety Actions:
•	HHLL (1300mm): Voting logic 362-003 trips to prevent liquid carryover to downstream equipment
•	LLLL (300mm): Voting logic 362-007 trips to prevent pump cavitation or loss of seal
