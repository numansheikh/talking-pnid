**2\. The Relief and Venting System (Top Nozzles)**

Look to the top of the vessel for safety and pressure monitoring.

**Nozzle N6 (Pressure Safety Valve \- PSV):**

* **Start:** A 3-inch Flange connects to the vessel.  
* **Line:** **3" PP01 361 GF 018 B 03 E7-P**.  
* **Reduction:** The line reduces from 3-inch down to 1-inch.  
* **Component:** **PSV-02** (Pressure Safety Valve).  
  * *Spec Change:* Piping class changes from 150 rating to 300 rating here.  
* **Discharge:** After the PSV, a reducer expands the line from 2-inch to 4-inch.  
* **Drain/Isolation:**  
  * The discharge line features a Ball Valve **(HV-26)**.  
  * Between the reducer and HV-26, there is a **1/2-inch Drain Valve (HV-27)**.  
* **Exit Line:** **4" PP01 500 VK 03 B 01 V8**.  
* **Destination:** Flare System **(NPG PP01 PRP ID 21001\)**.

**Nozzle N4 (Instrumentation):**

* **Component:** Connected to a 2-inch Solenoid Valve **(HV-34)**.  
* **Instrument:** Pressure Gauge **(PG-004)**.

**Nozzle N7 (Instrumentation):**

* **Component:** Connected to a 2-inch Valve **(HV-28)**.  
* **Instrument:** Pressure Gauge **(PG-003)**.

**Body Instrumentation:**

* Located physically between nozzles N7 and N9 is the Pig Signaler, tagged **ZD-04** (Field) and **ZI-004** (DCS).

 

**3\. The Small Bore Loop (Nozzles N1 & N9)**

This is a loop system likely used for kicking or equalizing, involving two specific nozzles.

**Nozzle N1:**

* **Size:** 2-inch.  
* **First Valve:** **HV-38** (2" Ball Valve).  
* **Line:** **2" PP01 361 GF 015 B 03 E7-P**.  
* **Injection Point:** *Crucial Detail:* Between the first valve (HV-38) and the second valve (HV-43), the line from **Nozzle N9** enters here.  
* **Second Valve:** **HV-43** (2" Globe Valve).  
* **Instrument:** Flow Element **F402** (Field Instrument).  
* **Third Valve:** **HV-96** (2" Ball Valve).  
* **Spec Change:** The line becomes **2" PP01 500 VK 02 B 01 B8**.  
* **Destination:** It connects to the Flare System line **(3" PP01 500 VK 01 B 01 B8)**.

**Nozzle N9:**

* **Size:** 2-inch.  
* **Valve:** **HV-42** (2" Valve).  
* **Line:** **2" PP01 361 GF 0018 B 03 E7-P**.  
* **Destination:** This line loops back and connects into the **N1 Line** (specifically between HV-38 and HV-43).

**5\. The Main Outlet & Barred Tee Complex (Nozzles N10 & N2)**

This involves the massive 20-inch and 16-inch piping leaving the vessel.

**Nozzle N10 (Main Scraper Outlet):**

* **Size:** 20-inch.  
* **Line:** **20" PP01 361 GF 0011 AP 15L-P**.  
* **Valves:**  
  * **HV-25** (20" Ball Valve).  
  * **HV-23** (20" Ball Valve).  
  * **Bleed:** Between these two is a **2-inch Drain Valve (HV-24)**.  
* **Destination:** This line flows into the **Barred Tee**.

**The Barred Tee (BT) Connection:**

* The Barred Tee merges the flow from the Scraper Receiver (N10) with the flow coming from the Scraper Launcher.  
* **Incoming from Launcher:** Line **20" PP01 361 GF 07 AP 15L-PP** (Details in Section 6).  
* **Branch Out:** A **16-inch line** exits the Barred Tee.  
  * Line: **16" PP01 361 GF 014 B 03 F9-P**.  
  * Valve: **MOV-002** (16-inch Control Ball Valve).  
* **Instrumentation on the 16" Line:**  
  * **Temperature:** **TE-02 / TI-02** (2" connection).  
  * **Pressure:** **PI-04 / PIT-04** (1" connection) fitted with Solenoid Valve **HV-46**.  
  * **Corrosion Monitoring:** 2" connections for **CC02, CP002, CIT2, CE02**.  
* **Destination:** This 16-inch line goes to the Fuel Gas Knockout Drum **(NPG PP01 PRP 0801\)**.

**Nozzle N2 (Bypass/Parallel Line):**

* This nozzle connects the vessel to the Fuel Gas system (joining the 16" line described above).  
* It splits into two parallel paths:  
  1. **8-inch Path:** Contains two **8-inch Ball Valves (HV-29 and HV-31)**. Line: **8" PP01 361 GF 017 B 03 F9-P**.  
  2. **2-inch Path:** Contains a **2-inch Ball Valve (HV-32)** and a **2-inch Globe Valve (HV-33)**.  
* **Termination:** These paths merge and connect to the 16-inch line headed to the Fuel Gas Knockout Drum.

 

**6\. The Incoming Line (From Scraper Launcher)**

This is the line feeding *into* the Barred Tee (upstream of the receiver).

* **Line:** **20" PP01 361 GF 07 AP 15L-PP**.  
* **Main Valve:** **EZV-02** (20-inch Control Ball Valve).  
* **Pig Signal:** Located between EZV-02 and the Barred Tee is **ZI-3 / ZS-03**.

1. **Flare Connection (3-inch):**

   * Instrument: **FO5** (Flow Orifice).

   * Final Line: **3" PP01 500 VK 01 B 01 B8**.  
   * Destination: Flare System **(NPG PP01 PRP 2101\)**.  
1. **Instrumentation Taps (2-inch):**

   * **Tap 2:** **HV-22** (2" Solenoid Valve) connecting to **PIT-03, PI-03**.
